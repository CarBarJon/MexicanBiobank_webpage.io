About Us

The Mexican Biobank is a pioneering initiative dedicated to understanding the genetic diversity of the Mexican population. By integrating genomics, bioinformatics, and clinical data, our mission is to promote equitable precision medicine and close the knowledge gap for underrepresented populations worldwide.

Projects

Population Genomics

Exploring genetic diversity across Mexican states to understand population structure and ancestry contributions.

Pharmacogenomics

Studying genetic variants that influence drug response to improve precision medicine in Mexico.

Health Disparities

Investigating how genetics contributes to health disparities and developing strategies to address them.
